About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at Zhongshan Hospital, Fudan University, advised by Prof. Junbo Ge (Academician of CAS) and Prof. Li Shen. My research sits at the intersection of Interventional Cardiology, Flexible Electronics, and Translational Medicine.

I specialize in large animal models (swine/canine) for preclinical validation of novel medical devices. My current focus is establishing the "Device-Vessel Suitcordance" framework to optimize the interaction between interventional devices and vascular tissues.
