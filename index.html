About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow and Scientific Secretary at the National Basic Science Center for Panvascular Interventional Complex Systems, Zhongshan Hospital, Fudan University, working under the mentorship of Prof. Junbo Ge (Academician of the Chinese Academy of Sciences) and Prof. Li Shen.

My clinical and research expertise lies at the intersection of interventional cardiology, flexible electronics, and bioresorbable medical devices. I am highly experienced in large animal models (swine/canine) and the preclinical in vivo validation of novel cardiovascular implants. My core research philosophy is focused on establishing "Device-Vessel Suitcordance" to improve panvascular patient outcomes.
