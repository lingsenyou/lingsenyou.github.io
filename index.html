About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at Zhongshan Hospital, Fudan University, working under the mentorship of Prof. Junbo Ge (Academician of CAS) and Prof. Li Shen.

My research vision is to bridge the gap between engineering innovations and clinical reality. I specialize in utilizing large animal models (swine/canine) to establish the "Device-Vessel Suitcordance" framework.

I have a profound interest in integrating cutting-edge technologies into panvascular interventions, particularly:

AI in Healthcare
Advanced Materials
Bioresorbable Electronics
Translational Models
